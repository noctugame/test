QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is NoctuGame?

NoctuGame is a revolutionary gaming ecosystem that combines elements of NFTs, a metaverse, and traditional gaming mechanics to provide a unique and immersive experience. Players can participate in a dynamic world where they can own, trade, and leverage assets within the game environment.

How can I participate in the NoctuGame presale?

You can participate in the NoctuGame presale by visiting our presale platform on PinkSale. Ensure you have a compatible wallet and follow the instructions on our website to secure your tokens early.

What benefits do NKT tokens provide?

NKT tokens allow players to purchase in-game items, participate in governance decisions, and access special events within the NoctuGame universe. Token holders can also stake their tokens to receive rewards and participate in exclusive content.

Can I trade NoctuGame NFTs on other marketplaces?

Yes, NFTs acquired within NoctuGame can be traded on our dedicated NFT marketplace as well as other compatible external platforms, allowing for greater liquidity and trading opportunities.

What measures are in place to ensure token security?

NoctuGame takes security seriously; all smart contracts related to the token and game are regularly audited by third parties. Additionally, we implement industry-standard security practices to protect token holders and players.
